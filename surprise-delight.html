Surprise & Delight: The Ultimate Loyalty Hack

The Concept: Unexpected bonuses trigger oxytocin and serotonin—our brains reward positive surprises with emotional loyalty.

I experienced this when a software vendor upgraded my account for free. The small gesture made me a raving fan.

How to Apply It

  • Random Upgrades: Offer occasional free add-ons—customers will remember the “gi.”
  • Personalized Notes: Handwritten thank-you cards or quick video messages from your team build real connection.
  • Milestone Rewards: Celebrate anniversaries or usage milestones with exclusive content or discounts.

Self-Reflective Angle

On my own journey, I started setting small surprise “treats” for myself—like a coffee date after completing a tough task. It boosted my morale and kept me moving forward.
